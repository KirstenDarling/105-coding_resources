Be my study buddy!

My name is Kirsten Darling. I'm a Skillcrush Bootcamp student. I work with HTML5, CSS3, Sass, and JavaScript. I spent 10 years managing retail stores in Missouri, Texas, Oklahoma, and Colorado before I found my passion: frontend web development. I enjoy mixing my retail/customer service skills with my tech skills on the websites I build. I absolutely love working with small business owners to help create their first website or make their existing website responsive (made for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices).

I created this website so I could share some of my favorite frontend development resources. This site includes links to some useful HTML, CSS, and Git resources as well as links to websites that can help with picking colors and layouts for your projects. I love discovering new tools just as much as sharing them. So, please email me if you have any resource suggestions for me to try out or add to this website. I appreciate your feedback. And, I love making new developer friends, so feel free to add me on social media or check out my code on Github.
